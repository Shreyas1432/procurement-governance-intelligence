# Chart 04: Contract Value Distribution

## What this shows
The log-scaled distribution of contract final prices.

## Why it matters
Reveals contract size skewness, helping scale financial inputs appropriately.

## Key findings
- Highly right-skewed distribution, typical of power-law spend.
- Median contract value is approximately EUR 64K.
- Validates logging contract values to resolve heteroscedasticity.

## Limitations
Outliers may be data entry mistakes rather than massive infrastructure works.
