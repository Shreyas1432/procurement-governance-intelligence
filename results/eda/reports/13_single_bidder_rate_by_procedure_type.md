# Chart 13: Single-Bidder Rate by Procedure Type

## What this shows
The percentage of awards with a single bid across procedure types.

## Why it matters
Correlates procedure selection with competition restriction risks.

## Key findings
- Open tenders exhibit low single-bid rates (around 8%).
- Negotiated tenders without publication yield single-bids in over 60% of cases.
- Supports procedure type as a strong feature for governance risk modeling.

## Limitations
Correlation does not imply collusion; direct awards naturally result in single bids.
