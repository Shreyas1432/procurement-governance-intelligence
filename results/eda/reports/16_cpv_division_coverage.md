# Chart 16: CPV Division Coverage

## What this shows
The distribution of contracts across top CPV divisions.

## Why it matters
Identifies the core public purchase areas (e.g., Construction, IT, Medical Equipment).

## Key findings
- Construction (CPV 45) and Medical/Pharma (CPV 33) dominate the record count.
- IT/Software services (CPV 72) represent a growing share.
- Supports sector-stratified anomaly filtering.

## Limitations
CPV codes are often broad and do not capture specialized sub-categories.
