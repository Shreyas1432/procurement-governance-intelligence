# Chart 03: Temporal Coverage Heatmap

## What this shows
The heatmap of field presence (non-nullness) across years.

## Why it matters
Pinpoints the exact years where critical variables are populated enough for panel data estimation.

## Key findings
- Price proxy and bid counts are fully populated from 2014 onward.
- Earlier records (pre-2010) show severe field gaps, particularly for award dates.
- Validates restricting ML experiments to 2014-2020.

## Limitations
Only measures presence, not validity or semantic correctness of field values.
