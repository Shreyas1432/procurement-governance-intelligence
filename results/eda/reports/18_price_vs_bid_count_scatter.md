# Chart 18: Price vs Bid Count

## What this shows
The correlation between contract final price and number of bidders.

## Why it matters
Tests the standard economic theory that competition lowers procurement costs.

## Key findings
- Shows a weak negative trend: higher bid counts align with slightly lower prices relative to estimate.
- Higher bids cluster around mid-value contracts.
- Supports price variance models that adjust for bid density.

## Limitations
High-value contracts have unique cost structures that mask price-bid relationships.
