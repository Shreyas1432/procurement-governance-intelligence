# Chart 11: Supplier Year-on-Year Activity

## What this shows
The count of active suppliers operating in the market per year.

## Why it matters
Acts as a proxy for supplier entry/exit barriers and overall market churn.

## Key findings
- Stable active supplier count post-2014, matching electronic filing adoption.
- High continuity suggests a mature vendor ecosystem.
- No massive exit waves detected post-2015.

## Limitations
Cannot distinguish between true market exits and temporary reporting gaps.
