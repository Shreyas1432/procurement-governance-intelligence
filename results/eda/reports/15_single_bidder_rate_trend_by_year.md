# Chart 15: Single-Bidder Rate Trend by Year

## What this shows
The temporal trajectory of the overall single-bidder rate.

## Why it matters
Evaluates if public market transparency and competitive pressure are improving over time.

## Key findings
- Single-bidder rate rose slowly during the 2014-2018 period.
- Indicates a slight decline in active competition despite platform digitization.
- Validates the need for systemic governance risk monitoring.

## Limitations
Influenced by changing reporting limits and thresholds for direct awards.
