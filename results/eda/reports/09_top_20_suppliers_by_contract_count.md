# Chart 09: Top 20 Suppliers by Contract Count

## What this shows
The most active suppliers by cumulative count of awarded contracts.

## Why it matters
Identifies dominant system vendors who could be systemic risks or network hubs.

## Key findings
- State-owned entities and massive utility firms lead contract volumes.
- Shows minor concentration within the top-10 vendors.
- Highlights critical nodes in the bipartite market graph.

## Limitations
Subsidiaries are not aggregated under their parent holding company.
