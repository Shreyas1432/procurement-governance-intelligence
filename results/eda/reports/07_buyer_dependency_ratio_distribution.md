# Chart 07: Buyer Dependency Ratio Distribution

## What this shows
The distribution of public buyer concentration with their top-5 suppliers.

## Why it matters
Exposes single-buyer capture vulnerability and potential market lockout risks.

## Key findings
- Over 23% of buyers exhibit dependency ratios greater than 0.5.
- Shows structural lock-in with a tiny vendor cohort.
- Confirms high exposure to supplier insolvencies.

## Limitations
High dependency ratio may reflect specialized equipment markets with single providers.
