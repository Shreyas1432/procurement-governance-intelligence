# Chart 08: HHI Distribution Across Buyers

## What this shows
The cumulative distribution of Herfindahl-Hirschman Indices (HHI) for buyers.

## Why it matters
Compares public market concentration against regulatory thresholds (e.g., DOJ/FTC guidelines).

## Key findings
- A substantial portion of buyers exceed the 0.25 highly-concentrated threshold.
- Suggests restricted competitive dynamics in local contracting.
- Matches regions with limited local supplier pools.

## Limitations
HHI is computed locally; does not account for multi-buyer framework agreements.
