# Chart 17: Price Distribution by CPV Division

## What this shows
The price range and distribution across the top CPV categories.

## Why it matters
Evaluates pricing heterogeneity across different industry verticals.

## Key findings
- Medical equipment exhibits tighter price distributions than construction works.
- Log variance highlights the necessity of sector-specific normalization.
- Confirms distinct economic profiles per sector.

## Limitations
Fails to capture raw unit-price variability.
