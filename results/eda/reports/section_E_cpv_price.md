# Section E: CPV & Price Summary

## Purpose
Examines CPV sector coverage, price ranges by division, and correlation between bids and contract price.

## SQL-Backed Charts
- `16_cpv_division_coverage`: Chart 16: CPV Division Coverage
- `17_price_distribution_by_cpv_division`: Chart 17: Price Distribution by CPV Division
- `18_price_vs_bid_count_scatter`: Chart 18: Price vs Bid Count

## Artifacts
PNG, HTML, Markdown, and metadata JSON files are generated and stored in `results/eda/plots/` for each chart.
