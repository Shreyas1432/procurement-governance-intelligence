# Chart 12: Procedure Type Distribution

## What this shows
The relative share of contracts across open, restricted, and negotiated procedures.

## Why it matters
Assesses compliance with transparency directives promoting open bidding.

## Key findings
- Open procedures represent the majority of higher-value contracts.
- Negotiated procedures without publication remain common in low-value brackets.
- Highlights procedure selection compliance variations.

## Limitations
Procedure codes can be misclassified during data entry.
