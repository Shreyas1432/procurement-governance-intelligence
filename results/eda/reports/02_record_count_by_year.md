# Chart 02: Record Count by Year

## What this shows
The annual volume of procurement contracts spanning 2006 to 2021.

## Why it matters
Identifies database reporting activity cycles, policy shocks, or systemic reporting gaps.

## Key findings
- Data reporting starts slow from 2006, peaking significantly in 2014-2015.
- Reflects the adoption of electronic reporting portals and digitalization mandates in Italy.
- Maintains a stable baseline of over 100K valid records per year post-2014.

## Limitations
Reflects reporting portal compliance rather than true macroeconomic changes.
