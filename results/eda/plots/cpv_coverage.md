# CPV Price Coverage

## What does this chart show?
This chart summarizes a SQL result from `pricing/cpv_coverage.sql` using 12 aggregated rows.

## Why is this important?
It turns procurement records into a reviewable signal for governance intelligence.

## Key findings
Review the highest bars, strongest trends, or largest flows first. These indicate where concentration, competition, risk, or pricing patterns are most visible.

## Business impact
The chart helps a procurement reviewer identify where attention should be focused before reading individual contracts.

## Research impact
The chart supports reproducible evidence for RQ3 and links the visual result to a named SQL query.
