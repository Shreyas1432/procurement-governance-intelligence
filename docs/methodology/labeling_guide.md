# Contract Risk Labeling Guide

## HIGH RISK

- Negotiated procedure without publication and single bidder
- Buyer dependency ratio > 0.7 and repeat same-supplier wins
- Contract amendments > 2

## MEDIUM RISK

- Negotiated or restricted procedure
- Single bidder or buyer dependency > 0.5
- One to two amendments

## LOW RISK

- Open procedure and multiple bidders
- Buyer dependency < 0.3
- No amendments
